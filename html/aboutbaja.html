About Baja


What we do…

The Baja SAE competition is an intercollegiate engineering design competition for all majors. The object of the competition is to simulate real-world engineering design projects and their related challenges. These challenges stretch far beyond the reach of engineering and require a broad base of students with various majors in order to be a complete team.
Each collegiate team is competing to have its design accepted for manufacture by a fictitious firm. Students must function as a team to design, build, test, promote and compete with their vehicle. These specific tasks, along with managing a student’s educational responsibilities, provide one of the most challenging but rewarding, experiences a student can have while enrolled at Iowa State.
